ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Skyward Centre is a Tuition centre located in Nairobi, Kenya, that has been operational since 2003.

With a pool of experienced tutors in various curricula, we pride ourselves with having assisted hundreds of students realize their potential and improve their grades exponentially.

Students are guaranteed one-on-one tutorial sessions with their tutors.

Skyward Centre addresses the needs of students who do Home Schooling and those who require extra tuition after school, on the weekends and during the holidays.




WORKING HOURS

We offer Home Schooling and extra tuition after school, on the weekends and during the holidays.For this reason, we are open every day of the week from 8:00am-8:00pm.




         

HOME SCHOOLING

The homeschooling program is primarily designed for students pursuing the British National
Curriculum (BNC), with the aim of taking the IGCSE O-Level and the GCE A-LEVEL
Examinations.

The classes are offered to students at the Skyward Centre on a one-on-one basis, where
every student is assigned his/her own tutor. We therefore do not conduct group classes.

Students are registered at the British Council For their final Cambridge examinations,or with
KNEC for their final Edexcel Examinations.

TUITION

Skyward Centre offers tuition classes to students pursuing both the IB and the British
National Curriculum (IGCSE, GCE).

Each tuition session lasts 1hour 30 minutes.

For the IB program students, the tuition classes span right from PYP years to the completion
of the IB program (IB1 and IB2)

For the British National Curriculum, the tuition classes address the needs of students in all/
the Key stages (Key Stage 1-4), and then AS (Advanced Subsidiary) and A-Level (Advanced Level).
